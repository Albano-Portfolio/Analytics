Turning Data Complexity Into Clarity

I'm a Senior Healthcare Data Analyst and Business Intelligence professional based in Nashville, TN, with over 18 years of experience at the intersection of healthcare operations, advanced analytics, and emerging AI technology.

At The Cigna Group, I serve as a Business Analytics Advisor and Claims/Data SME, leading complex analytics initiatives across Total Medical Cost, provider performance, utilization management, and cost containment. I build executive dashboards, automate reporting workflows, and deploy AI-enabled tools to surface insights that directly influence strategic decision-making.

My edge is the rare combination of deep healthcare domain expertise — spanning claims, quality, HEDIS, fraud detection, and value-based care — with modern technical fluency in Databricks, SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, and AI tools like Databricks Genie, Microsoft Copilot, and Cursor AI.

I hold a Stanford Certificate in AI in Healthcare, a Vanderbilt Certificate in Generative AI, and active certifications from Databricks, Tableau, Snowflake, and Microsoft. I believe the future of healthcare analytics is AI-augmented — and I'm building it today.
